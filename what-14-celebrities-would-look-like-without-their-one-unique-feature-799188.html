What 14 Celebrities Would Look Like Without Their One Unique Feature

Sometimes, life can be harder for celebrities because their fans and haters are prepared to pick apart their appearances for days on end. But the stars from our compilation clearly don’t pay attention to comments like these, and they’ve turned their alleged “flaws” into unique features that we can’t imagine them without. Photoshop can work wonders, so let’s fantasize about what our favorite celebrities would look like without their distinguishing traits.

We at Bright Side were very impressed with the results and are anxiously awaiting your opinions in the comments.

Adele

Adele’s famous chin with the dimple in it is the singer’s distinctive feature (aside from her divine voice, of course), so we decided to see what would happen if this feature was removed. Clearly, Adele is always beautiful.

Tom Hiddleston

The handsome Tom Hiddleston would cease to look like himself if you lowered his hairline and changed the distance between his eyes. These changes may seem like nothing, but they transform the face so much.

Mila Kunis

It’s impossible to look away from Mila Kunis’ large eyes. But to be honest, she didn’t look any worse after we made her eyes a bit smaller.

Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez looks adorable with her chubby cheeks and that feature doesn’t change anything. Even if we gave her high cheekbones and a sharp chin, she’s still pure magic.

Will Smith

We honestly didn’t change anything except for Will Smith’s ears, and we were shocked by how much the actor’s face changed. Turns out nature was wise when it gave Smith this feature.

Dita Von Teese

It’s hard to argue that the incredible curves of the burlesque queen are her forever signature trait. However, even without her breathtakingly narrow waist, Dita looks gorgeous.

Tilda Swinton

Tilda Swinton is a brilliant actress who knows how to play both male and female roles. And we wondered what would happen if we made a more feminine version of the actress. Let’s be direct — all her charm has disappeared.

Cate Blanchett

Did you even notice what we changed about the charming Kate Blanchett? Yes, they performed some kind of “rhinoplasty” and we regretted it immediately.

Lily Collins

The full eyebrows on this actress are her signature feature, and it seems to us that there’s a reason for it.

Keanu Reeves

It’s hard to imagine the incredible Keanu Reeves with a different hairstyle, but we tried. It’s up to you to judge the results.

Blake Lively

After we removed Blake Lively’s famous squint and beauty mark, and we can say, with confidence, that something is clearly missing from her look.

Jennifer Aniston

It’s hard to imagine Jennifer with different hair color, but there’s something to it. We wonder what Brad Pitt would say to this.

Bella Hadid

Bella Hadid doesn’t lose her charm, even without her famous bow lips, although, it seems to us that her look is missing something.

Ian Joseph Somerhalder

Ian Somerhalder has incredible blue eyes. But even with brown ones, his look doesn’t lose its charm, does it?

What celebrity transformation surprised you the most?
