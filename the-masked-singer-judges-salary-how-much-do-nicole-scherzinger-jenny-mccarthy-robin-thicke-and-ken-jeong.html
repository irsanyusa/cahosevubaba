Who are you? That’s the phrase that everyone utters when they watch an episode of The Masked Singer and are dying to know which celebrity will be unmasked. The judging panel offers diverse opinions and comedic commentary that make the show fun and exciting. While it is not exactly clear how much each judge is making per episode, we can get a better idea by taking a look at their estimated net worths.

Jenny McCarthy on ‘The Masked Singer’

Jenny McCarthy is always shocked every time a contestant is unmasked. Are you shocked to learn that she has an estimated net worth of $15 million dollars? Her 25-year career has seen a multitude of television appearances. Up until this past year, she has been a correspondent on Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve special. This coupled with her radio show and multiple books that she’s put out have no doubt brought in some big bucks for her. With her appearance on season three of The Masked Singer, her net worth has definitely risen from its estimated $14 million in 2019.

Robin Thicke on ‘The Masked Singer’

It may be shocking to know that Robin Thicke is presumed to be the highest-paid judge. He also has an estimated net worth of $15 million. His lengthy music career makes him provide his music expertise when trying to make guesses on the show. Robin has collaborated with a ton of artists including season three contestant Lil Wayne. He is also a prominent songwriter which also explains his riches. His appearance on The Masked Singer comes after he was under fire for his song “Blurred Lines” which sparked a debate about toxic masculinity.

Ken Jeong on ‘The Masked Singer’

Ken adds the comedic flair to the panel and is notoriously known for his outrageous guesses which are almost always wrong. Ken has an estimated net worth of $14 million. He may be a licensed physician, but Ken got his claim to fame from the show Community, as well as playing Lesley Chow in the Hangover franchise. It is worth mentioning that he correctly guessed that The Poodle mask in season one was Tori Spelling so he deserves a little credit.

Nicole Scherzinger’s Salary on ‘The Masked Singer’

Nicole is no stranger to being a reality television show judge. It may be shocking that she has a $12 million estimated net worth even though she is on three different reality shows on three different continents. The recent revival and tour of the Pussycat Dolls may also play into an increase of her net worth. She has been in show business for nearly two decades and even got her start auditioning on the reality television show Popstars before eventually joining the band Edens Crush. There’s no doubt that Nicole has conquered both the music world and the world of reality television and it doesn’t seem like her career will be slowing down any time soon.
