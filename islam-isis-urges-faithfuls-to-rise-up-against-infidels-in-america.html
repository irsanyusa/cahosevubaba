ISIS urges faithfuls to rise up against infidels in America

As a follow up, they promised the faithfuls that they would bring them opportunities to serve the group from wherever they are, even if they are banned from travelling to join ISIS.

A statement from an ISIS spokesperson made available to Reuters says that they have already started spreading their campaign across the globe, citing the posting of the pictures and addresses of 100 supposed 100 U.S military personnel.

The ISIS spokesperson said they are also going to hack into the secured database of nations involved in the coalition against its caliphate to get the names and personal details of military personnel in other nations that are against the ISIS ideology for its brothers in the faith to participate in the jihad.
