Squirrel Girl
New York City’s Central Park has finally found the hero it deserves. With enhanced strength, speed and a fuzzy tail to boot, her most fearsome ability may be her ability to command a legion of squirrels at will. Just don’t leave any macadamia nuts lying around or she’ll come nipping at you.
