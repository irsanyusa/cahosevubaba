BBNaija's Phyna Celebrates 27th Birthday With Stylish Photos, Turns Heads, Many Celebrate Her

  • A former BBNaija reality star Phyna looked gorgeous as she wore two classy outfits for her 27th birthday
  • The former reality star, who is known for her controversies online, took the opportunity to appreciate herself
  • Her colleagues and fans were wowed by her stunning looks and wished her a wonderful birthday on Instagram

A former Big Brother Naija reality show housemate, Josephine Otabor, aka Phyna, is celebrating her 27th birthday on Sunday, June 2, and she took a moment to hype herself.

For her first set of pictures, she wore a dashing purple dress that gave her a tiny waist and highlighted her curves.

The gown was made with extra fabric, which was attached to her waist and extended to the ground.

She wore a brown and silver outfit for her next photoshoot session. The short dress exposed her curves and made her look like a diva.
