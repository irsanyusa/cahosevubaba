15 Times Our Eyes Deceived Us With Their Vision

What we see isn’t always real, Inception taught us that, after all. However, in real life, people also encounter optical illusions where they least expect them. Fortunately, folks find the phenomenon of seeing a full-size baby human or a 2-D backpack pretty fascinating and share it online for the amusement of others.

Bright Side challenges you with a few baffling photos that require power to be understood.

1. Curly-haired person or a sea urchin...

2. “My dog didn’t lose his head.”

3. “This pic my aunt took of a sunrise at her office building makes my head hurt.”

4. “It looks like my bridesmaid wore a white sleeve on one arm.”

5. “I almost have a heart attack whenever I walk into the kitchen at night because of my shadows and this cat.”

6. “A full-size baby human”

7. “The truck looks like it has a possum logo.”

8. “Just a cow. Its name is Bertha.”

9. This empty microwave isn’t actually hollow.

10. “A giant Labsaurus Rex”

11. “Top-down view of a store — I found it pretty confusing when looking at the image.”

12. “One of my student’s backpacks”

13. “I literally just asked my coworker’s jacket if it was feeling all right.”

14. “On the Internet, nobody knows you’re a cat.”

15. “The goblin under the staircase”

Which pic did you find the most confusing? Have you ever come across any glitches in the matrix? Test us with your own photos in the comments!
