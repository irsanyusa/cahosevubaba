We Ranked 12 of the Hottest Celebrity Bachelors, and Were Eager to Hear Your Opinion

Hollywood is full of heartthrobs whose talent is equally impressive as their good looks, but sometimes even that doesn’t make it any easier to find “the one.” Some of these guys may have a very unique take on marriage, and others are not ready to fully commit, but somehow, that doesn’t make them any less attractive. In fact, it’s a challenge to decide which one of them is more desirable. Make sure to keep scrolling to the end to see if your celebrity crush has made it to the list.

12. Alexander Skarsgård

Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News

Although the handsome Swedish superstar actor has had a number of relationships with famous women, he has never been married. He and his current girlfriend welcomed their first child just recently, but it’s still unknown if marriage is on the table for this 45-year-old stud.

11. Drake

The good-looking rapper has been linked to numerous gorgeous female stars, from Rihanna to Tyra Banks, but it looks like he hasn’t settled yet. Drake is currently enjoying his successful career and being a dad to Adonis, a son he has from a relationship with a French model and actress Sophie Brussaux.

10. Jon Hamm

AUDE GUERRUCCI/AFP/East News

The Mad Men star might be a bachelor, but he knows a thing or 2 about being in a committed relationship. He dated actress Jennifer Westfeldt from 1997 to 2015, but they never made it official. “To me, people get married when they’re ready to have kids, which I’m not ruling out,” Hamm said at the time.

There have been rumors that Hamm could possibly tie the knot with his current girlfriend, Anna Osceola, in the near future.

9. Colin Farrell

Kristin Callahan/Everett Collection/East News

The closest Farrell has gotten to tying the knot was back in the 2000s, when he and English actress Amelia Warner allegedly said ’’I do’’ in a non-legal ceremony. Sadly, the marriage lasted only a couple of months. But the bachelor lifestyle doesn’t prevent the handsome Irish actor from being a father to 2 boys from his previous relationships.

8. John Boyega

The British actor and producer is currently single, and it seems that there is no emotional commitment on the horizon, since Boyega is trying to focus on his career at the moment. But since his dating life news is always in the spotlight, the public will probably be among the first to know the latest developments in Boyega’s love life.

7. Harry Styles

Invision/Invision/East News

The charismatic musician and actor seems to be enjoying his single life to the fullest. The 29-year-old Styles has been in a number of high-profile relationships, the most public one being with actress Olivia Wilde, but he has remained happily single. Considering his age, we don’t expect to see him settling down anytime soon.

6. Jared Leto

Despite rumors about his engagement to Cameron Diaz, the 51-year-old Leto has stayed true to his bachelor lifestyle. “I don’t believe in settling down. The term implies that the current path I’m on isn’t valid. It’s not always about living your life the way people have lived before you,” the blue-eyed heartthrob once explained.

5. Scott Eastwood

AFP/EAST NEWS

After tragically losing his girlfriend in 2014, the son of the legendary actor, Clint Eastwood, has been in a couple of relationships, but he has remained unmarried. He admits that it was difficult for him to get back into the dating game. We hope that the future brings more luck to his personal and romantic life.

4. Robert Pattinson

Cyril Pecquenard/KCS/East News

Robert Pattinson has been through some ups and downs in his dating life, but he has not given up on searching for his perfect match. He’s been dating an English model and actress named Suki Waterhouse for a while now, but the couple is trying their best to keep their relationship private. There have even been some engagement rumors, and we’re hoping that Waterhouse will be ’’the one’’ for Pattinson.

3. Bradley Cooper

Invision/Invision/East News

The Hollywood superstar has been linked to many famous beauties, including Renée Zellweger and Olivia Wilde. He was married to actress Jennifer Esposito back in 2006 for just a couple of months and has a 5-year-old daughter from his relationship with model Irina Shayk, but it seems like getting married is still not on his agenda.

2. Zac Efron

JA/Everett Collection/East News

The charming actor has never been married and is currently single. In an interview from October 2022, Efron revealed that although he’s taken a break from the dating scene, he is optimistic about finding love, adding that he’ll probably meet the right person when he least expects it.

1. Chris Evans

Invision/Invision/East News

Chris Evans’ love life has sparked curiosity among his fans ever since he dated Jessica Biel from 2001 to 2006, which was his longest and probably the most serious relationship known to the public. After their split, Evans had more than a couple of flings and relationships, and he’s currently dating a gorgeous Portuguese actress, Alba Baptista. The couple seems happy despite their 16-year age gap.

What do you think — which one of these famous bachelors is the closest to saying ’’I do’’? What other single celebrity guys do you believe deserve to be on this list? Let us know in the comments.
