How does Christmas impact people's mental health?

published on August 17, 2024
Over two in five Brits have felt stressed during the festive season, while about one in four has struggled with anxiety or depression.December is a happy time for most. Even as the days get shorter and the Christmas music starts to grate, two in five say their mental health improves during the holidays.
