Tartan Day in the USA

published on July 17, 2024
WHAT IS THE NYC TARTAN DAY PARADE?  The annual NYC Tartan Day Parade showcases the contribution Scots have made to the USA. It brings together clans, bands, community groups and people across the US who celebrate their connection to Scotland.  The parade strikes a universal chord from international pipers and drummers, being loud and proud to celebrate the Scottish contribution made to America.   Last year, 3,600 people took part in the NYC Tartan Day Parade and it was watched by over 45,000 spectators.
