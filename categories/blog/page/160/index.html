Bruce McAvaney to Lead ABC's Olympic Games Coverage

published on July 18, 2024
Bruce McAvaney will carry the torch for the ABC’s Olympic Games coverage, while Roy & HG will return with daily hi-jinks from the competition. One of the most familiar voices in sportscasting, McAvaney will lead a team comprised of former Olympians Melinda Gainsford-Taylor (Athletics), Sarah Ryan (Swimming), Simon Orchard (Hockey), Brad McGee (Cycling), ex-Davis Cup player John Alexander (Tennis), Matilda Amy Harrison (Football), and experienced broadcasters. Highlights from the state-funded broadcaster’s coverage of the 2024 Paris Olympic Games will include a daily ritual from Roy & HG, the comedy duo whose 2000 Sydney Olympics spot “The Dream” became a global sensation.
