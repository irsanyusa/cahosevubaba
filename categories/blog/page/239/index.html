Names of God in Igbo

published on June 20, 2024
The names of God in Igbo language are numerous. This ethnic group has a rich culture, which is shown in everything. People praise God, thank Him, follow Him. Learn these extraordinary names and their meaning, maybe you’ll find something new and precious for yourself.
