Princess Diana Is the Most Attractive Royal of All Time, According to Science

Princess Diana appeared to be one of the top 5 most attractive royal women, according to a golden ratio, which was developed by the ancient Greeks to measure physical perfection. The late princess outshines Queen Rania of Jordan, Kate Middleton, and Meghan Markle.

We at Bright Side were left in awe by these results, and we’d love for you to find out how Diana’s perfection was calculated. And in the bonus feature, we will unveil who is considered to be the most attractive male actor of all time — you don’t want to miss it.

The ancient Greek “golden ratio” was applied to conduct the experiment.

A plastic surgeon named Dr. Julian De Silva used the digital version of the golden ratio in his research and applied the standards to famous women. The results came out to be surprising: Diana took first place in the ranking of the 5 most well-known royal women. Her face shape, including the size and position of the lips, nose, chin, and jawline appeared to be the best match to the ideal ratio, which states that the ideal face should be about 1.6 times longer than it is wide. The distance between the forehead hairline and the bottom of the chin should be divisible into 3 equal parts.

Diana outshined Meghan Markle and Kate Middleton but had another strong rival in this royal contest.

Diana was found to be an 89% match to the golden ratio. Dr. De Silva commented on the results: “Diana made the highest score for her face shape, her nose width, her brow area, forehead, and eyebrows. Her lowest scores were for her chin and lips. Her lips are a bit thin and not so well-defined and her chin is less ideal than some of the other women.”

Meanwhile, Queen Rania of Jordan was found to be the most beautiful living royal with a rating of 88.9%. “She scored the highest of all for her chin which is the closest to ideal and also for her lips. She showed lower results for her nose width and her brow area,” says De Silva.

Princess Grace Kelly took third place.

The former Hollywood star and Princess of Monaco earned a score of 88.8%. She scored the highest for her eye spacing, which appeared to be nearly ideal with a score of 99.8%. Her lips also won the highest score.

Meghan Markle and Kate Middleton took the fourth and fifth places, but each of them has outstanding features on their own as well.

Meghan Markle scored 87.4%, which put her in fourth place. But experts praise her highly for her almost perfect facial symmetry with an ideally shaped nose and chin that are the closest to the Greek ideal standards of all time.

Kate Middleton closed the “top 5” list of the most attractive royals with a score of 86.82%. However, the “perfect gap” between her nose and lips and strong eye spacing were considered by experts to be the closest to perfection.

Bonus: The same rule was applied to define the most attractive man and the result is quite surprising.

According to the golden ratio rule, the most attractive man in the world scored 92.15% and left his rivals far behind. And no, it wasn’t Leo Di Caprio. We at Bright Side know who this was and you can find out too by reading this article.
