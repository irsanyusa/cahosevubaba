Rosy Meurer and Churchill Olakunle celebrate their son as he turns 1

"My dear son, in Truth your mum brought me happiness and filled a vacuum in my heart, that’s why I named you ‘Omoniyi- The meaning (A child that brings you honour)," Churchill wrote.

"Your birth brought me great joy and gladness. You are the patch of green in the desert of my existence and the spitting image of me %100. Sometimes, my emotions could be controlled whenever I behold your presence and I wonder how lucky I am that I get to be your Dad."

For Rosy, she described her son as her anchor, king and blessing to their family.

"My Anchor! My king! My heart! My everything! ❤️ it’s been exactly a year since our lives have been wonderfully spiced with your special presence. You are a special gift from God, may you increase in flavour, honour and grace all the days of your life," she wrote.

"May you grow to be an exceptional young man; you shall excel more than your parents in Jesus name. My precious jewel of immeasurable value, it shall be well with you in Jesus name."

Happy birthday to Omoniyi from all of us at Pulse.

They welcomed their first child together a few months after they announced their marriage.
