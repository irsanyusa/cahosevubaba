Dr. Pimple Popper Pulls Out A Massive Lipoma From A Woman's Back In New Youtube Video

In her latest , you can think the giant lump on her patient's back is a run-of-the-mill lipoma. The bump is massive, and if you liked her jiggly lipoma removal , this is a truly extraordinary lipoma. One fan asked: "Is this a pimple or an extra organ?" Good question.

Dr. Pimple Popper adds a lengthy description to match this feature-length pop: "We've got a bundle of joy popping out in this Lipoma Bebe on the back." The Instagram video is a teaser for the full-length Youtube video.

She continues with a full-on lipoma lesson. "Lipomas are slow-growing, benign (non-cancerous) growths of fat cells. These cells are contained in a thin capsule that creates the appearance of a bump right under the skin. Lipomas are typically not tender, move around easily, and dont require treatment.

Not everyone, including this patient, wants to hold onto their lipoma. In that case, Dr. Pimple Popper can remove it without too much trouble. "Often a small incision can be made over the lipoma and they can be 'popped' out easily, which is a simple in-office surgical procedure under local anesthesia," she says.

When Dr. Pimple Popper dives in for this removal, she makes the incision and extends it longer and longer. Then, she takes hold of the lipoma and works to free the fatty mass. Free at last, what a relief for the patient and all the patient fans.
