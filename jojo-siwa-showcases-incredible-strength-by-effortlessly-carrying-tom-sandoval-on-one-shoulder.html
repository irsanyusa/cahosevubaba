JoJo Siwa Showcases Incredible Strength by Effortlessly Carrying Tom Sandoval On One Shoulder

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In the latest episode of Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test, JoJo Siwa’s remarkable display of strength took center stage. The reality series, known for pushing celebrities through grueling military-style training, reached a pivotal moment in its second season. This time, Siwa stepped up in an unexpected way that left viewers pleasantly surprised with her physical strength.

JoJo Siwa Carried Tom Sandoval on Special Forces

The episode featured a demanding challenge called the “commando cliff crawl.” At an altitude of 4,600 feet above sea level, the recruits faced a daunting task. They had to traverse a single rope suspended over a ravine, requiring them to crawl across, let go halfway through, and then pull themselves back up to complete the test. However, only Sandoval and Olympic gold medalist Bode Miller managed to conquer this difficult feat successfully.

For the rest of the group, including Nick Viall, who came very close to succeeding, the consequence for their failure was a taxing punishment. They were tasked with carrying Sandoval and Miller one mile back to their camp. While some struggled with this physically demanding task, Siwa stepped up in a remarkable way.

During the challenging journey, Viall, clearly struggling with the weight of his failures, passed Sandoval off to Siwa. Known for her vibrant and glittery persona, the 20-year-old sensation effortlessly slung the 40-year-old grown man over her shoulders and carried him the remainder of the way. Siwa’s incredible determination and drive were on full display for all to see.

“Everything I’ve gotten in my life has been because of my work ethic,” Siwa said. “I want the world to see that side of me—that determination, that drive.”

There Are Only Seven Contestants Remaining on the Show

After a two-week delay, Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test returned with a new episode. The reality series is halfway through its second season, and seven contestants remain.

Among the remaining contestants are Jack Osbourne, Bode Miller, Siwa, Sandoval, Viall, Tyler Cameron, and Erin Jackson. Seven other celebrities have already voluntarily withdrawn from the season, including Blac Chyna, Tara Reid, Brian Austin Green, Dez Bryant, Robert Horry, Savannah Chrisley, and Kelly Rizzo.

Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test airs on Monday nights at 9/8c on Fox, and viewers can look forward to more intense challenges and inspiring moments as the season continues.
