Season 9 of The Masked Singer has been full of surprises ever since the premiere! And this week, we get one of the most FUN theme weeks ever, Sesame Street Night! Plus, two all-new singers are taking on the Squirrel.

But who is the celebrity under the Jackalope costume? And what clues do we have about them? Here’s everything we know!

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 9

Jackalope’s clues began with her on stage, claiming she loves “entertaining audiences of all ages.” She said she’s known to have a lot of “influence,” but wasn’t always very social. Talking as a kid “wasn’t her jam,” she explained as she held up vines. Growing up, she expressed herself through music and dance. We see what looks like a TikTok or social media frame on the shot.

A clapboard comes in with “MYSTERY” written on it. Pretty soon people all over the world began connecting with her! Two men in black walk up wearing headbands and sweatbands. This Jackalope “don’t lie!” She created a “modern family of fellow outcasts.” One of the men in black holds a red heart necklace with a “G” on it. Jackalope claims to have a thick skin, then we see a framed photo of the Scream mask. Her moves “shine brightest on Wednesdays.”

After her performance, Jackalope said that she’s partied with Nicole Scherzinger once. Then, Count Von Count gave a clue that said “30 under 30.” She explained that she was on the same list as Donald Glover, Emma Stone, and Michael B. Jordan.

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Jackalope

Nicole seemed to think she knew who the Jackalope is. She predicted that it’s “her girl” Lele Pons! Guest panelist Jennifer Nettles thought it might Camila Cabello. Jenny guessed it was Jenny Ortega, based on her connection to Wednesday and the Scream franchise. Online, a lot of fans agreed with Nicole that it was Lele Pons or that it could be Jenna Ortega or Sara Hyland. All these options were brought up by the judges, so they must be on to something this week!

Who is the Jackalope on The Masked Singer?

After going head-to-head with the Fairy in the Battle Royale, the Jackalope was eliminated. And she was revealed to be…

Lele Pons!

And after a handful of amazing performances, time with the Sesame Street characters, and more, this was such a fun way to end the night! And Lele really showed up on that stage. We’re sad to see her go! But we’ll get two brand new singers to take on the Fairy next week. Stay tuned on The Masked Singer!
