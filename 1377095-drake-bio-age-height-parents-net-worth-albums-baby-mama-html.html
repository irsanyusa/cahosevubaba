Drake biography

This rapper, who is also a singer, a songwriter and a businessman, was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. His full birth name is Aubrey Drake Graham.

Drake parents, Dennis and Sandra, are African-American and Ashkenazi Jewish Canadian respectively. Like his son, Dennis was a musician back in the day - he played drums.

When Aubrey was only five years old, his parents split up, and he remained with his mother in Toronto, while his father went to the United States. The young man has had a strained relationship with his father, who struggled to support him financially and was involved in legal trouble.

Aubrey started his career as an actor when he was 15 years old. He portrayed the role of Jimmy Brooks on "Degrassi: The Next Generation", a Canadian teenage drama series.

However, he later decided to connect his life with singing, drawing inspiration from his idols Jay-Z and Clipse. This led to him releasing his debut mixtape, titled "Room for Improvement" in 2006.

The talented youngster signed to Young Money Entertainment, and in 2010, his debut album "Thank Me Later" came out. It instantly brought him great success, debuting at number one on the United States Billboard 200 chart.

His next commercial success was with his second album "Take Care", which came out in 2011, and the third one "Nothing Was the Same", which was released in 2013. Two years later, his mixtape "If You're Reading This It's Too Late" saw the world, solidifying his fame.

The fourth album "Views" broke the records, staying on top of the Billboard 200 chart for a total of thirteen weeks. Drizzy became the first male artist in over a decade to have his album top the chart for such a long time.

In 2017, he put out his second commercial mixtape "More Life", which once again became record-breaking. The next year, his album "Scorpion" went public, and it contained three singles that later became number one in the United States.

His third mixtape "Dark Lane Demo Tapes" and sixth album "Certified Lover Boy" were released in 2020. Once again, they established Drake as a world-famous artist who always thrives to surprise his listeners.

This artist has won a lot of prestigious accolades, including four Grammy Awards. Among his other achievements are 27 Billboard Music Awards, six American Music Awards, and three Juno Awards.

His entrepreneurship career includes being one of the founders of the OVO Sound record label, as well as being appointed as the global ambassador for the Toronto Raptors NBA team. He also took part in the development of Virginia Black, the bourbon whiskey brand.

Drake's influence on rap music and popular culture can not be underestimated. He is often in the headlines, whether it is for releasing an album, signing profitable deals, feuding with someone, or dating a celebrity such as Rihanna or Jennifer Lopez.
