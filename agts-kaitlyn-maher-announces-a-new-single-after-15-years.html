'AGT's Kaitlyn Maher Announces a New Single After 15 Years

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

NBCUniversal via Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In a recent Instagram post, America’s Got Talent sensation Kaitlyn Maher announced the release date for her upcoming single, “Good Friend Salary.” The post not only revealed the long-awaited launch date, but also provided insights into the inspiration behind the song and a glimpse into Maher’s creative process.

Kaitlyn Maher Announces Release Date For Her New Single

The anticipation is clear as Maher’s loyal supporters eagerly await the release of her first single in 15 years. This milestone marks a new chapter in her musical journey, one that promises to be as heartfelt and authentic as her previous work.

For fans and followers of Maher, the single provides a personal connection to the artist’s journey and the emotional depth embedded in her music. “Good Friend Salary” appears to touch upon themes of self-worth, healthy boundaries, and the delicate balance between being a compassionate friend and preserving one’s own emotional well-being.

Maher’s music career initially took flight on the AGT stage when she was just four years old, where her powerful voice and undeniable talent left a lasting impression. Now, as she gears up for the release of “Good Friend Salary,” fans also have the opportunity to witness her evolution as an artist.

“Good Friend Salary” will be dropping on Friday, September 15, and will be available on all major music platforms.

Kaitlyn Maher Shared a Snippet of The Lyrics

Maher also shared a sneak peek into her new single “Good Friend Salary” by sharing a portion of the song’s touching lyrics. These lines convey the essence of the track, showing emotional exhaustion, friendship imbalances, and the impact of giving too much without receiving in return.

With lines like “So go take your problems somewhere else, Cause I’m doing therapy for free” and “Those two am chats are getting kinda old,” Maher provides a raw and relatable perspective on the challenges of maintaining relationships, all while staying true to her signature lyrical depth and emotional resonance.

The lyrics resonate as a powerful reminder of the importance of setting healthy boundaries and prioritizing one’s own well-being in relationships. Maher’s ability to blend her personal experiences with universal sentiments promises to strike a chord with fans, making “Good Friend Salary” a song that not only entertains but also inspires self-reflection and empathy.
