She Won Miss Universe Many Years Ago and at 76 Still Looks 30

Fast-forward to today, at the age of 76, Apasra Hongsakula still looks astonishingly young and full of life, as if time hasn’t touched her at all. She was Thailand’s first Miss Universe winner.
