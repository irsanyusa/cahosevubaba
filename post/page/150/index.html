The 5 best techno clubs in Berlin

published on July 22, 2024
Over the last thirty years, Berlin has become internationally recognized for its uniquely vibrant club culture. For many years if you said you were moving to Berlin you would be asked; “so are you a DJ or an artist?” The DJs might tell you they are both, and once you get to know Berlin’s techno scene, you will agree. Beloved, notorious, fantastical and scandalous, Berlin’s club culture is such a singular feature of the city’s character that there is even a movement evolving to have Berlin’s techno culture recognized by UNESCO as intangible cultural heritage.
