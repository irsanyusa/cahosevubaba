John Legend Drops First Episode of 'Trailer Talk' Season 3 Featuring Niall Horan

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

John Legend has dropped the first episode of the third season of his popular YouTube talk show, Trailer Talk, featuring Niall Horan. In the episode, Legend and Horan chat about a variety of topics, including Horan’s recent season on The Voice, his new music, and his friendship with Legend.

John Legend’s Trailer Talk Returns With Niall Horan as the First Guest

The first guest of Trailer Talk‘s third season is Legend’s fellow coach on The Voice Horan, who is welcomed as a “favorite Irishman” by the host. They kick off the episode by discussing Horan’s victorious coaching experience in Season 23 of The Voice. Horan’s humor and charm shines through as he reflects on the season and the absence of fan support for Team Blake, the last season for the show’s beloved coach, Blake Shelton.

Horan’s journey from the UK’s X Factor to becoming a global superstar is a remarkable one. Legend shares a personal connection with Horan and the members of One Direction, having met them when they were still teenagers on X Factor. Horan reflects on his teenage years, revealing that he was just 17 at the time, and how incredible it is to look back on that experience.

The duo also discussed the evolution of Horan’s songwriting skills. Horan admits that his early attempts at songwriting weren’t great, but he persevered, learned, and refined his craft. His songwriting experience with One Direction also comes into the conversation, revealing that the band collectively wrote songs, sometimes voting on the best ones.

Horan delves into his transition to a solo career and shares his songwriting approach in his interview with Legend. He embraces his unique style and highlights the importance of his fans. He highlights the significance of having a dedicated fan base from his One Direction days and the inspiration he found during the pandemic. This inspiration then led to his latest album, The Show.

John Legend, Niall Horan Performed a Duet on Trailer Talk

The episode then culminates with a beautiful duet performance of the title track from Horan’s album, The Show. The song reflects on the unique challenges and realizations brought about by the pandemic, reminding us of the value of life’s ups and downs.

As Trailer Talk Season 3 kicks off, Legend and Horan set the stage for engaging and insightful conversations with more celebrities and musicians in the episodes to come. The show continues to provide viewers with an authentic and intimate look at the lives of their favorite stars.

The talk show offers a refreshing take on celebrity interviews. With Legend’s inviting demeanor and his guests’ candid stories, it promises to be a delightful watch throughout Season 3.
