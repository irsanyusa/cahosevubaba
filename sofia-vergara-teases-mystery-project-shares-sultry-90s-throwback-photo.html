Sofia Vergara Teases Mystery Project, Shares Sultry '90s Throwback Photo

Modern Family star Sofia Vergara’s schedule is seemingly jam-packed. The Colombian actress recently posted an image of her filming, teasing her latest mystery project. Vergara also delighted fans when she uploaded a sexy throwback photo to promote her newest suncare product.

Sofia Vergara Teases Mystery Show with Raw TV Set Snaps

This week, Vergara revealed on Instagram that she’s working on a new project. In her social media update, the America’s Got Talent judge gave a sneak peek of her upcoming mystery show.

In the post, Vergara was wearing a skintight navy-colored dress, emphasizing her amazing physique. Surrounded by cameras and equipment, she was sitting on a gray couch overlooking large floor-to-ceiling windows. Vergara also went with full-glam makeup to complement her caramel-colored hair styled into light waves.

“Set day Miami in the 90’s comming soon,” she wrote.

Upon seeing her teaser, fans quickly went to the comment section to express their excitement. Several users praised how ageless the Colombian star looks even at the age of 52 while someone asked if she is filming a new series.

Vergara’s post comes after the Modern Family actress was seen enjoying a date night with orthopedic surgeon Justin Saliman. The two were seen spending a romantic evening in Los Angeles.

According to the doctor’s online bio, Saliman has an Ivy League education, as he completed a fellowship at Stanford University. He also finished a medical degree from the University of Colorado School of Medicine. Interestingly, he reportedly invented a “revolutionary meniscus repair device” and owns a medical startup.

On Thursday, Vergara took her millions of followers in awe after she shared two stunning bikini throwback. While promoting her new product Transforma, the Colombian bombshell posted her alluring photo back in the ‘90s.

In the Instagram post, the actress uploaded image of herself wearing lacy black lingerie while propped in the sand. Vergara also posted a snapshot of her voluptuous backside as she stood topless while facing the ocean. In the photo, Vergara was wearing an embroidered white thong while her long hair flowed over her chest.

“The reason why I’m religious now about using Transforma at 51. All those years of unprotected sun in the 90’s !!!” she wrote.

On Wednesday, Toty’s Instagram account revealed the look of their new product. The post also wrote a short informative description alongside the photo.

“Your total transformation of sun damaged skin is here,” the caption read. “This holy grail serum reduces dark spots, decreases fine lines and wrinkles, smooths the skin’s texture and more! The mixture of AHA’s and HA’s, retinoid, and niacinamide corrects photoaging. It’s the perfect addition to your PM skincare routine.”
